Frequently Asked Questions

What is the dress code?

We’re going for “City Cocktail” Think cocktail attire in bright or neutral colors Gentlemen: dress shirts, suits optional. Ladies: mid or full-length dresses.

When is the RSVP deadline?

Kindly let us know by August 15, 2025. You can RSVP on this website on our RSVP page.

Can I bring a plus-one?

Only guests who have “and guest” on their invitation may bring an additional guest. If your invitation doesn’t list a plus-one, we hope you’ll still join us solo!

What kind of food will be served?

We’ll have a station style dinner with options for everyone! Our stations themes will be Surf & Turf, French Quarter, and Picnic

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely—please let us know on your RSVP form if you have any allergies or dietary needs (gluten-free, nut-allergy, vegetarian, etc.) so we can pass it along for our caterer to prepare accordingly.
